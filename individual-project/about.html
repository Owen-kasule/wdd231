About Techrooot

At Techrooot, we’re rooting sustainable technology in African communities—one digital solution at a time.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by Owen Kasule Muhereza, Techrooot was born from a desire to bridge the digital divide in Africa. Inspired by the resilience and ingenuity of local entrepreneurs, we set out to create AI‑driven platforms that streamline healthcare, enrich education, optimize agriculture, and drive business growth.

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment through Technology – We believe tech should uplift individuals and communities.
  • Sustainable Growth – Solutions designed for long‑term impact, not quick fixes.
  • Community First – Local needs guide our design; we listen before we build.

Our Mission

  • Lifelong Learning
  • Resilient Communities
